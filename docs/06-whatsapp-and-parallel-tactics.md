# WhatsApp Business + parallel tactics — the Morocco channel mix

> In Morocco, WhatsApp is the primary B2B conversation channel. Email is the
> opener; the close happens in a green chat thread. Build everything else
> around that reality.

## WhatsApp Business setup

### Day 1 setup

- [ ] Use a dedicated SIM (not personal). Inwi B2B or Maroc Telecom Pro
      lines — keep this number for years; don't renumber.
- [ ] Install **WhatsApp Business** (free) on a dedicated phone or via
      Business multi-device.
- [ ] Profile filled in:
  - Name: `Mediterra Fresh`
  - Catégorie: `Alimentation / Service de livraison`
  - Description (FR): `Fournisseur B2B de fruits et légumes frais pour
    hôtels, restaurants, pâtisseries et riads. Marrakech & Agadir.`
  - Address: same as `lib/site.ts`.
  - Hours: same as site.
  - Email + website: linked.
- [ ] Greeting message ON:
  > Bonjour ! Merci de nous écrire. Notre équipe vous répond sous 30 minutes
  > en jour ouvré (Lun-Sam 8h-18h). Pour une commande urgente : appelez
  > directement le +212 6 00 00 00 00.
- [ ] Away message ON:
  > Nous sommes fermés. Nous traitons votre demande dès l'ouverture demain
  > matin à 8h. Bonne soirée — l'équipe Mediterra Fresh.
- [ ] **Quick replies** configured for the 6 most-asked questions:
  - `/livraison` → liste des zones et créneaux
  - `/minimum` → seuil de commande par ville
  - `/paiement` → conditions (comptant, virement 15j/30j)
  - `/saison` → calendrier de saison du mois
  - `/echantillon` → procédure pour recevoir un panier d'échantillon
  - `/catalogue` → lien `mediterra-fresh.com/catalogue` + PDF

### Catalogue feature (essential)

- [ ] Build the WhatsApp catalogue with **20 hero SKUs** (top sellers).
      Each item: photo (square 1:1), name, MAD HT/kg, packaging, calibre.
- [ ] Group catalogue products by collections: `Agrumes`, `Fruits rouges`,
      `Herbes`, `Légumes feuilles`, `Saisonniers`.
- [ ] Refresh once per week — pin the most in-season product to the top of
      the collection.

### Broadcast lists (consent-only)

- [ ] Maintain **one broadcast list per ICP** (`broadcast-hotels`,
      `broadcast-patisseries`, `broadcast-riads`).
- [ ] Add a contact only after **explicit opt-in** during the first
      conversation:
  > Souhaitez-vous recevoir notre catalogue de saison une fois par
  > semaine sur WhatsApp ? Réponse simple : "Oui" pour recevoir.
- [ ] Save consent date + the message in your CRM sheet.
- [ ] Broadcasts: **Tuesday + Friday morning, 7:30**, max 2 per week.
- [ ] Each broadcast has a clear opt-out line:
  > Pour ne plus recevoir : répondez STOP.

### Volume rules to avoid getting your number flagged

- Max 256 contacts per broadcast list (WhatsApp limit anyway).
- Max 20 outbound messages per minute (Business app caps you).
- Never message the same number more than 1× per 48h unless they replied.
- If a contact has not replied to 2 broadcasts → remove from the list.

## Sample basket — the Morocco conversion ritual

The single highest-ROI tactic in this market. Spend 60 % of week-3 effort
here.

### What goes in a basket

For a **hotel 5★** (target package value: 350-400 MAD raw cost):
- 2 kg oranges Maroc Late
- 1 kg avocados Hass (calibre 14)
- 500 g strawberries Sabrina
- 250 g raspberries
- 1 botte mint
- 1 botte coriander
- 1 head romaine lettuce
- 1 product traceability sheet (date, origin, producer name)
- A4 catalogue printout (FR + EN)
- Card with WhatsApp QR + sample tariff

For a **pâtisserie** (target package value: 200 MAD):
- 500 g strawberries
- 250 g raspberries
- 250 g blueberries
- 4 mangoes
- 1 sample of citron Eureka
- Catalogue + WhatsApp QR card

For a **riad medina**:
- 1 kg oranges
- 5 small avocados
- 1 botte each of mint, parsley, coriander
- 500 g cherry tomatoes
- 1 head lettuce
- Catalogue card

### Drop-off protocol

1. **Call/WhatsApp 24h ahead** — confirm a time slot, ask name of F&B
   Manager / sous-chef / owner.
2. **Arrive on time, in branded apron + cool box** — visual signal
   matters more than you'd think.
3. **Hand the basket directly to the decision-maker**, not to security
   or reception. If they're unavailable, leave a sealed cool box at the
   pass with the chef + send a WhatsApp *with a photo* of the bag at
   the pass.
4. **Don't ask for a meeting on the spot.** Say: "Goûtez ce soir, je vous
   relance demain matin." Then leave.
5. Next morning at 8:30, send a single WhatsApp: *"Bonjour {{name}}, alors,
   verdict ?"*. That's it.

### Conversion expectation

- 10 baskets dropped → 5 chefs taste them seriously → 3 reply to your
  follow-up → 2 ask for pricing → 1 first order in week 4.
- 10× higher conversion than email alone, but 10× the effort. Both are
  needed.

## Instagram + Facebook playbook (12 weeks)

| Week | Posts | Reels | Theme |
|------|------:|------:|-------|
| 1 | 3 | 1 | Brand intro: who we are, where we source |
| 2 | 3 | 2 | Cold room tour, packing line, daily delivery |
| 3 | 3 | 2 | Behind the scenes at a partner farm in Souss |
| 4 | 3 | 1 | Close-ups of the top 5 SKUs (food porn macro) |
| 5 | 3 | 2 | Client kitchens (with permission, tagged) |
| 6 | 3 | 1 | Seasonal calendar of the month |

### Content rules

- **No stock imagery, ever.** Chefs spot it in 1 second and lose trust.
- One person on the team owns IG; post Tue + Thu + Sat at 8h00.
- Tag the producer in every post about a SKU.
- Reply to all DMs in < 4 hours; redirect commercial questions to WhatsApp.
- 70 % of content is product/process; 20 % is people; 10 % is offers.
- Geo-tag every post (Sidi Ghanem, the farm location, the client kitchen).

## Google Business Profile

- [ ] Verify by postcard (Google sends to Sidi Ghanem; takes 14 days).
- [ ] Photos: 25 minimum at launch (cold room, van, products, team).
- [ ] Categories: `Wholesale grocer`, `Produce wholesaler`,
      `Fruit and vegetable wholesaler`.
- [ ] Service area: Marrakech + Agadir + 30km.
- [ ] Hours: same as site.
- [ ] Posts: 1 per week (offer, new SKU, seasonal calendar). GBP rewards
      consistency.
- [ ] Encourage **3 first reviews** in week 1: ask three friendly chefs
      who've already tried a sample basket. Reply to every review within
      24h, signed with a real name.

## Trade events — the ones that actually matter

| Event | When | City | Why |
|-------|------|------|-----|
| **SIAM** (Salon International de l'Agriculture au Maroc) | Late April | Meknès | Source side: meet new producers + GlobalGAP-certified partners |
| **Horeca Morocco** | June | Casablanca | Demand side: F&B Managers from across Morocco |
| **Salon de l'Hospitalité Marrakech** | November | Marrakech | Local hotel buyers |
| **Sirha Morocco** | Biennial, autumn | Casablanca | Chef-focused; gastronomic positioning |

### Booth essentials

- Branded cool box visible from 5 m away.
- 6 SKUs to taste (cut on the spot — citrus + berries + herbs work best).
- Tariff sheet pre-printed in FR + EN.
- WhatsApp QR code laminated A5.
- Lead sheet (paper) — capture name, role, venue, WhatsApp, urgency in 30
  seconds. Don't ask for "email"; ask for "WhatsApp".

After the event: same-day WhatsApp message to every lead, simple sentence
referencing the booth meeting:

> Bonjour {{first_name}}, c'était un plaisir au {{event}} ce matin. Je vous
> envoie la fiche tarifaire ce soir comme convenu. Bonne journée — Salim.

## Channel-mix reality check

| Channel | Volume | Conversion | Cost / acquired client (MAD) |
|---------|-------:|-----------:|----------------------------:|
| Cold email | 600 mails / mo | 0.5 % to client | 200 |
| Sample basket drop-off | 25 baskets / mo | 10 % to client | 800 |
| WhatsApp inbound (referral) | 30 / mo | 25 % to client | 100 |
| Instagram inbound | 10 / mo | 15 % to client | 300 |
| GBP "call" | 15 / mo | 30 % to client | 0 |
| Trade event | 30 leads / event | 10 % to client | 1500 |

> Lesson: **GBP + WhatsApp + sample baskets are your best ROI**. Email and
> trade events bring volume and brand exposure but cost more per signed
> client. Don't let an outreach campaign distract you from polishing the
> Google Business Profile and the basket protocol.
